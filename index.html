Data Science Project Showcase

End-to-end data pipeline & visualization app in the context of an heavy industry for my MSc thesis.

Motivation

The strategic goals in industries in heavy mature industries, with low product differentiation is through price competition. Harnessing the power of data driven solutions to increase the efficiency of processes and machinery yields much more interesting results than investing into new expensive equipment, specially during credit crunch periods. The value proposition is thus to capture real time data from industrial programmable logic circuits through the shop-floor network and store it into structured databases, to compute industry relevant metrics and to build a dashboard to support managerial decisions.

Deliverables

  1. Data pipeline with emphasis in data validation
  2. Develop a data validation tool
  3. Create a structured database
  4. Build a data visualization dashboard

Problems

  1. Machines generations had up to 50 years age gaps
  2. Poor data integrity at source
  3. Time-memory tradeoff & solution complexity optimization
  4. Communication barriers between stakeholders

Final Remarks

  1. Agile methodologies are more suitable for these projects
  2. Spend more time planning & interacting with key users, workers, and less time coding
