#Negotiation Master

The negotiation master data is extracted from the SAP registries. The current process relies on manual entries using old SAP interface to collect the data about part numbers, payment terms, suppliers & suppliers quota, supplied plants, price, consigment and incoterms. This procedure is time consuming and error prone since often times each supplier has tens of part numbers each with different supplier plants & terms. Multiple SAP systems & views are required to get the overview over just a part number.

The current proposal leverages SAP oracle and builds a masterfile from a streamlined procedure taking as input the part numbers.
