Price Negotiation

Simple tracking dashboard for 2024 annual price negotiation based on kanban negotiation cards

Motivation

The annual price negotiation is an important end of year event where buyers receive their targets from their business units to achieve strategic objectives. This is a call for action to anchor suppliers, evaluate leverages and plan strategies to achieve the best contract terms for the following year(s). This dashboard is a tracking tool for the negotiation status for all buyers in Braga. As input, it reads the informations in standardized excel nego-cards developed for such purposed. The main tracking targets are:

  1. scheduling of negotiations and timeline
  2. general overview of how Braga buyers are doing vs their benchmark
  3. get an individual overview for each buyer

Deliverables

  1. Nego-card templates
  2. Build a data visualization dashboard

Final Remarks

  1. Nego-cards should be kept simple with the minimum information to increase information accuracy and adherence
  2. Streamlit is an easy-to-use dashboarding tool but not very flexible when it comes to custom components
